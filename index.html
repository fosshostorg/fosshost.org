A message of condolences

Our former director, and long-time Arch Linux contributor, Jonathon Fernyhough, passed away on the 7th of January, 2023. The Fosshost team and community send our deepest condolences to his family and friends. You will be missed, Jonathon.

Fosshost to Sunset Tenant Services

At this time, Fosshost is deeply sorry to announce we are no longer able to continue offering our services.

Because of this, we strongly recommend all Fosshost tenants to backup their data immediately, and migrate elsewhere as soon as possible.

Update: A Message from our Founder

Fosshost's founder and CEO has a message for our community going over the challenges the organization faced leading up to this decision and discussing the future for Fosshost.

Where to Go

While Fosshost is sunsetting its hosting solutions, others in the community — including some of our former volunteers — who care deeply about free software sustainability have begun building alternative homes for free and open source projects who need the support.

The Radix Project

The Radix Project is committed to providing free-of-charge need-based hosting on request for your FLOSS/open source endeavors.

The Radix Project is one such initiative, founded in part by one of the original technical architects of Fosshost.

Read a message from their founder here.

Other Solutions

These organizations offer free solutions or usage for open source projects, and may be beneficial to tenants migrating off of Fosshost:

  • GitLab — GitLab offers either SaaS or self-hosted access to their "Ultimate" tier commercial GitLab package, including 50,000 CI/CD minutes, for qualifying open-source projects. Read more here: https://about.gitlab.com/solutions/open-source/join/
